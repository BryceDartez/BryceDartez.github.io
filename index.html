Who are we?

Esports LSU is an organization founded as a club in Baton Rouge, Louisiana's beloved Lousiana State University. We strive to be the home for Louisiana's greatest Collegiate esports players, and help our members to explore their opportunities in professional esports.

Competition

Esports LSU is a competitive group of people, and we prove ourselves at every event we attend. From Smash to Fortnite, we are home to some of the most winning collegiate esports athletes in the nation.

History

Esports LSU was founded on the basis of giving the esports community of Louisiana a gaming home during their years in college.

Community

Esports LSU takes pride in not only our home-grown gaming community, but also assists in the form of charity events to allow our members to be part of something bigger.
